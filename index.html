Canvas Day 1 Ready Checklist

This checklist is designed to help you prepare your Canvas Learning Management System (LMS) courses for the first day of school, ensuring a consistent and effective experience for students and families. Canvas is the required learning management for MISD making it the primary vessel for delivering the majority of digital classroom instruction.

I. Awareness for Family Access

II. Course Homepage & Basic Course Info.

MISD Canvas Templates have been created with a homepage that provides basic course information in a uniform format for students and families.

*Note: You can copy this information from previous courses & paste it.

*Students should access course content through Modules not pages/assignments.

III. Course Modules

*It is suggested that this module stay at the top for the first 4-9 weeks.

*Suggestion: Place the most recent modules at the top, or under the welcome module.

IV. Student Enrollment & Rosters

  • Ensure that you see all sections (periods) of your course.

*Students will be automatically rostered into Canvas at the beginning of each term. Courses with multiple sections (periods) and the same Course Code are combined into 1 Canvas course automatically.

*They will be removed during the nightly sync. Schedule changes will automatically update the roster every 24 hours.

V. Calendars

From the left side account menu click Calendar. You will see a calendar with your name which is viewable only to you, and you have calendars for each course. There is also an MISD calendar that reflects the Districts Academic Calendar.

  • The colored boxes to the left of the calendar names are your visible calendars.
  • You can change the colors of these calendars by clicking the 3 dots to the right.
  • Hide/show calendars any time by clicking the colored box. Gray boxes mean you won’t see events/assignments for those calendars.
